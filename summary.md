# Summary

The goal of our digital inheritance is to establish a flexible and decentralized framework that performs basic and advanced functions for automated digital value transmissions, fulfilling the need of different type of wills. Our decentralized application enables access to one's valuable crypto assets by selected entities in some pre-determined conditions. It is built on Ethereum, the most advanced and utilized smart contract platform in existence, with the largest developer community and the most advanced wallet infrastructure.&#x20;

#### Indexing via TheGraph

TheGraph is a decentralized infrastructure for indexing off-chain data such as the list of inherited wills.

#### Two different flows

* Inheritance: adding beneficiaries as multisig users of a Safe wallet based on pre-defined schema (required signatures) after a specified duration of inactivity.
* Forwarding: sending assets to beneficiaries' wallets based on pre-defined allocation after a specified duration of  inactivity.

#### Web3 Connect options

The following types of connections are supported:\
Metamask, Wallet Connect, Ledger, Trezor, Rainbow, and Coinbase Wallet.

#### ENS all around compatibility

Ethereum addresses are displayed by their assigned ENS if applicable, in all areas of the dApp.

#### Network switch capability

Allow the selection of main or test networks (e.g Sepolia) within the dApp, so that users can test the concept before connecting their real wallets.
